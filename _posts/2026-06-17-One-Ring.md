"One Ring to rule them all, One Ring to find them, One Ring to bring them all, and in the darkness bind them."

Sometimes the WestCoast feels like Middle-Earth. We’ve got the dramatic landscapes of the misty mountains right on our doorstep. And just as Frodo had to make a perilous journey, we have a quest ahead: deciding the future of local government.

In our case, this website <https://haveyoursay.westcoastcouncils.co.nz/>. is the key to ruling our rates, roads, and council services. If you don’t submit your input now, you’re basically leaving your voice behind at the Cracks of Doom—lost forever in the lava of bureaucracy!

Don't let the Orcs (or worse, apathy) take over. Whether you're a Hobbit who just wants a quiet life or an Elf wanting better infrastructure, this is your moment. Jump on the website and make a submission. It takes less time than a walk to Mordor! Make yourself heard before the darkness binds our future!
